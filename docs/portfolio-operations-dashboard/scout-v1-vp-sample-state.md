### What changed

### What is driving it

GKP is being driven more by conversion weakness than demand weakness, which means the problem appears to be execution or follow-through rather than pure traffic shortage. Citrus Ridge remains relevant because delinquency and NOI pressure are still material.

### Where to focus next

Focus first on Baymeadows and GKP. Confirm whether approved applications, scheduled tours, and renewal exposure support recovery this month. Then validate whether Citrus Ridge's financial drag is stabilizing or compounding.

- Review Baymeadows pending approvals and near-term move-out exposure.
